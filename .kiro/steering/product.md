# TradingAgents-CN Product Overview

## What It Is
TradingAgents-CN is a Chinese-enhanced multi-agent stock analysis learning platform based on the TradingAgents framework. It uses AI large language models (LLMs) and multi-agent collaboration to perform stock research and strategy experiments.

## Core Purpose
- Educational and research platform for AI-powered stock analysis
- NOT for live trading or investment advice
- Supports A-shares (China), Hong Kong, and US markets

## Key Features
- Multi-agent analysis system with specialized analysts (market, fundamentals, news, social media)
- Multi-phase analysis workflow with debate and risk assessment
- Support for multiple LLM providers (DeepSeek, DashScope/Qwen, OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, etc.)
- Real-time stock data synchronization from multiple sources (Tushare, AKShare, BaoStock)
- Batch analysis queue system
- Professional report export (Markdown/Word/PDF)
- User authentication and role management
- Favorites and watchlist management

## Target Users
- Chinese-speaking developers and researchers
- AI/ML practitioners interested in financial applications
- Students learning about multi-agent systems and LLM applications

## Important Disclaimer
This is for research and education only. AI predictions are uncertain. Investment decisions should involve professional financial advisors.
